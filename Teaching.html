Teaching Highlight — EMAI4EU

Starting 2025/26, I am teaching short modules in the EMAI4EU program (EIT Digital):

  • Ethics for Trustworthy AI (short version) — foundations of trustworthy AI, EU AI Act landscape, risk management, and practical checklists for research and deployment.
  • Introduction to Emotion AI (short version) — signals and sensing (speech, vision, physiology), model evaluation pitfalls, and privacy‑aware deployment at the edge.
