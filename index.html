About

I am a final-year PhD candidate in Information Engineering & Computer Science at the University of Trento. I work on communication-efficient and trustworthy federated learning for edge intelligence and TinyML. My focus is aggregation schemes, privacy-aware pipelines, and deployment frameworks for resource-constrained systems.

Research Areas: Federated Learning • Trustworthy ML • Edge AI • TinyML • Wireless/IoT Sensing
