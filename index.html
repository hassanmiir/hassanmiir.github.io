Bio

I am a final-year PhD candidate in Information Engineering & Computer Science at the University of Trento, Italy. My research focuses on communication-efficient and trustworthy federated learning, with applications to edge intelligence, TinyML, and smart environments. I design aggregation schemes, privacy-aware pipelines, and deployment frameworks for resource-constrained, real-world systems.
