About

I work on communication‑efficient and trustworthy federated learning with a focus on system‑level precision, reproducibility, and practical deployment at the edge. I develop methods and frameworks such as GASPU, FedEdge, FedFor, and PA‑FL to make distributed learning robust and usable in the wild. My interests sit at the intersection of distributed systems and machine learning: how to orchestrate resource‑constrained devices, compress and secure the learning signal, and evaluate end‑to‑end behavior with rigorous, reproducible pipelines. I enjoy building things that translate well from paper to production, and I’m keen on collaborations that push evaluation beyond accuracy into reliability, privacy, safety, and sustainability. I’m currently based in Trento, Italy, finishing my PhD at the University of Trento under Prof. Giovanni Iacca. From 2025, I will also contribute short modules within EMAI4EU (EIT Digital) on Trustworthy AI ethics and Emotion AI.
