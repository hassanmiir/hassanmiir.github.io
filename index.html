About

I am a final‑year PhD candidate in Information Engineering & Computer Science at the University of Trento, Italy. My research focuses on communication‑efficient and trustworthy federated learning, with applications to edge intelligence, TinyML, and smart environments. I design aggregation schemes, privacy‑aware pipelines, and deployment frameworks for resource‑constrained, real‑world systems.

Research Interests

  • Communication‑efficient federated learning: selective updates, GA‑based parameter selection, sign‑based aggregation.
  • Trustworthy ML at the edge: privacy, robustness, fairness, and monitoring in decentralized settings.
  • TinyML deployments: energy‑aware training/inference on microcontrollers and embedded devices.
  • Edge–cloud orchestration and hierarchical FL for smart buildings, IoT, and cyber‑physical systems.
