About

I work on communication-efficient and trustworthy federated learning with a focus on system-level precision, reproducibility, and practical deployment at the edge. I develop methods and frameworks (e.g., GASPU, FedEdge, FedFor, PA-FL) for scalable, real-world intelligent systems.

Research Areas: Federated Learning • Distributed Systems • Edge Computing • Internet of Things (IoT) • Blockchain
